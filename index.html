Hi there. My name is Kateryna Tanska. I’m a hair stylist based in San Francisco, CA.

Every Girl Is Unique

Let’s admit it. Girls are different. And so you are. I know that better than anybody else. That’s why my goal is not creating just beautiful hairstyle but making a hairstyle that makes you beautiful.

When I’m working on a hairstyle I consider many factors like your hair type, body shape, face shape and your outfit. It helps to create look that feels finished and coherent.
